WELCOME TO RED LION. REDEFINE.

Purpose

At our core, we’re creative capitalists who want to challenge the status quo. Never satisfied with existing patterns and routines, we challenge the norm to create new branded behaviours. We reinvent. We redefine. Welcome to Red Lion.

Values

It’s easy to say that we’re different. But we don’t just talk about our values, we actually live them every day. It’s with a passionate spirit of invention and constantly challenging ourselves to create something truly original, that we behave. And because creativity knows know limits, we have a lot of fun doing it, too.

Behaviours

Brands are measured by how they behave, rather than what they say. At Red Lion, we try to adhere to that practice. From passion projects to charity work, creativity symposiums to (many) laughing fits brought on by animal videos, we have created a culture that leads by example. Because without dedication, without active questioning, no behaviour is strong enough to challenge, and eventually change the status quo.

Rituals

Sure, we grab pints after work, but we believe that building and maintaining a cohesive team takes deeper work. Our Red Lion rituals express our cultural story and reinforce shared values. While we don’t hold hands and sing kumbaya, we understand the importance of refueling our internal engines daily. Whether it’s experiencing an art show together, testing out the latest tech as a team or having a chalk art contest (incriminating evidence below), we’re constantly engaging with and challenging each other.

Cues

Sometimes we need reminders that help keep us in touch with our purpose. Little cues that bring us back to the big picture after marathon work nights filled with pizza parties. From larger symbols, like our custom neon sign, to more subtle ones, like having free reign to fill our space with whatever we want (bikes, plants, derelict Halloween props, etc.), these physical expressions reflect our culture of redefinition and prompt inspiration.
